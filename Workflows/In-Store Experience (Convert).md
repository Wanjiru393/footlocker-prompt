# Section 02: In-Store Experience (Convert)

Business Function: Retail Operations & Training
**Trigger:** New product arrival in inventory or a scheduled staff training/briefing event.
# Prompts in this section: P04, P05

### Section Purpose
Speed to Floor:
Automates the creation of high-quality product descriptions for digital price cards and online listings as soon as stock arrives.

Sales Readiness:
Converts technical product specs into conversational "sales scripts" so floor staff are ready to upsell immediately.

Brand Voice:
Ensures that whether a customer reads a tag or speaks to a staff member, the language is energetic and benefit-led.

### Workflow Chain
New product arrival / Stock inventory update
│
▼
P04  Product Description Writer (Used for website & digital price cards)
│
▼
P05  Staff Upsell Script (Used in daily team "huddles" and briefings)
