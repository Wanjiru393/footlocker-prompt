# Section 01: Promotions

Business function:Marketing and Promotions
**Trigger:** Campaign event or promotional period that has been confirmed by a marketing manager in the campaign calendar.
# Prompts in this section: P01,P02,P03

### Section Purpose
Weekly Campaign Execution:
Foot Locker runs frequent promotions, including seasonal sales, product launches (Nike, Jordan, Adidas), and FLX member exclusives.

Consistency Across Channels:
The prompts ensure that promotional emails, social media captions, and post-campaign reports remain consistent in tone and branding.

Efficiency for Lean Teams:
By automating these tasks, the drafting time for a small marketing team is reduced from several hours per month to a quick review process.
