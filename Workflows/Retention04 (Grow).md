# Section 04 — Loyalty & Retention (Grow)

Business Function: CRM & Loyalty (FLX Rewards)  
**Trigger:** Independent data triggers (e.g., a customer reaches a point threshold or has been inactive for 90+ days).  
**Prompts in this section:** P09, P10

### Section Purpose
Increases customer spend by reminding members how close they are to their next FLX reward tier or "Status" level.

Targets "lapsed" customers (90+ days since last purchase) with low-pressure, high-value win-back offers to bring them back to the brand.

Scales personalized communication (using names, point balances, and specific product interests) that would be impossible to write manually for the entire FLX database.

### Loyalty & Retention Workflow Chain

CRM data trigger (Points threshold or Lapsed 90+ days)
│
├───► P09 · FLX Loyalty Reward Offer (Triggered by point balance/tier progress)
│
└───► P10 · Win-Back Email (Triggered by inactivity duration)
